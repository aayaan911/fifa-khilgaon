About Pre-Tournament Friendlies

These warm-up matches let teams finalize tactics and squad selections before the World Cup begins on June 11, 2026. Most matches are part of the "Road to 26" series hosted across the USA, Canada, and Mexico — the three host nations.

Key storylines: Messi's 6th World Cup, Haaland's first, host nation USA testing against Germany, and debutants like DR Congo getting final reps in.

🏆 Knockout Bracket — FIFA World Cup 2026

48 teams → 12 groups → 32 advance → 6 knockout rounds → 1 champion

Top 2 from each group + 8 best 3rd-place teams qualify. Bracket fills in as group stage completes.

📋 How 32 of 48 Teams Advance

48 teams in 12 groups of 4 · 72 group matches (Jun 11–28)
24 automatic: 1st + 2nd from each group (12×2)
8 best 3rd-place: ranked by pts, GD, GF, fair play, FIFA ranking
32 teams enter knockout stage · Single elimination · Extra time + penalties if drawn

12 groups (A–L) · 4 teams each · 3 matchdays per group
